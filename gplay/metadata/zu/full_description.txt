*** Game play ***
Siza izilwane Roll ukuba ukudla kwabo. Monkeys udle ubhanana, ama-apula kanye ithini lamapheya, namabhere udle salmon noju. Wood amabhlogo kukhona Breakable ngenkathi amabhlogo zamatshe akuzona. Kunezinhlobo ezingaphezu umdlalo izinto ezizayo, ne-game imiyalelo njengoba lo mdlalo iqhubeka.
